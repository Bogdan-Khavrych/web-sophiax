about us

Sophiax AG is a growing online business, which is considered a trusted marketplace by many. We are here for people to list, discover and buy new and genuine products. Sophiax AG is a parent to 3 linked e-commerce businesses.

what we do

At Sophiax AG we are committed to providing the best to our clients. We serve both businesses and direct to consumers. Our clientele is broad as we are working with both B2B and B2C clients. Whether you are an individual consumer or a business, we value our customers and provide them with the best of products. It is all about the convenience we offer in the name of our products.

There’s a lot that we have to offer under the Sophiax umbrella. The linked businesses offer a wide range of amazing products like sports equipment, baby products, and stationery items like journals. We are partnered with real estate projects as well as holding some of our own. We follow the modern trends in the world of E-commerce. We are glad to say that Sophiax is a hub for creative designers and skilled product managers who combine efforts to put together wonderful product designs.

Sustainability
and Growth

Sophiax AG wants to help create a more eco-friendly future by increasing investments in sustainable projects. By 2025, the company aims to turn many products into Eco-friendly versions and make eco-friendly products mainstream. Sophiax AG seeks to partner with Eco-Minded entrepreneurs and companies that need an investor with experience, skills, and relationships to scale profitably.

Eco-friendly industries use sustainable practices and reduce negative environmental impacts. Eco-friendly products have managed to reduce the negative environmental impact of the industry. The demand for eco-friendly products is growing, as people become more aware of the negative impact of the products they use.
